If you are entrepreneur, investor or organisation in the infrastructure, healthcare or education sectors looking to set up shop in India, Sunil Tandon can advise on the key aspects of your business. 

He has set up the country’s first private port, roadway, SEZ, airport and defence shipyard from concept to completion, Sunil is an expert in financial structuring and project management for large scale infrastructure projects. Having spent the first half of his 36 year career working with the government, Sunil is perfectly placed to guide companies entering the Indian market through the maze of Indian bureaucracy. 

During this time he has built relationships with international organisations such as the World Bank and IFC, worked with the French government, and built joint ventures with the likes of SAAB Sweden, Malaysian Airport Holding, Port of Singapore, Maersk Denmark, Fraport Germany, and AMP Australia.
